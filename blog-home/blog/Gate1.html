Sprint wise Review

Since i have started preparing full fledged since April 2021, I have divided each deadline to sections containing 2 weeks. By the end of these two weeks, I will review and reveal my success and failure scenarios and tweak it in the upcoming sprints

Sprint1: The beginning Date (March end-April)

Plan: Study every morning + wake up at 5:30 daily (Sleeping for 8hrs a day)!
Subjects: C-Programming and Data Structures. I learnt about Abstract Data Types and revised through my concepts.
Failure: Study was limited to only mornings because of my work..
Improvement Notes:

  1. My priority is Gate, so I need to plan every other actions like work, dance, blogging and so on by making time for it away from my dedicated time for studies. This change in mindset is mind-blowing.
  2. By Tracking your goals, one makes progress. So my next sprint should take care of this.

Study Technique

I like to study but I realize my big mistake is not staying consistent so i rely on my short-term memory for exam preparation. So I understand the fears of not being able to recollect subjects after the exam is over. Now I am changing it.

  • Retrospective Revision: Blank piece of paper, write down whatever you know about the subject
  • Active Recall: Just Recollect any topics
  • Spaced Repetition: Because my intention is to have a long term memory retention, i need to frequently recollect the subjects I have already studied.
  • Solve as many sums as I can get my hands-on: Since this is an exam I need to do this diligently.
